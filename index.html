About the Lab

We are dedicated to pioneering new computational methods to accelerate the discovery of effective drugs. Our lab integrates cutting-edge machine learning techniques, high-performance computing, and open-source principles to improve the drug discovery process.

Our Research Focus

  • Machine learning-based drug discovery
  • Open-source drug development tools
  • Computational models of disease mechanisms
  • Drug repurposing for rare and neglected diseases
